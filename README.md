# Arduino-Based Smart Food Service System
I’m excited to share my Minor Project titled “Optimising Food Service Efficiency”, completed as part of my B.Tech in Electronics & Communication Engineering at National Institute of Technology Andhra Pradesh.

🔍 Problem

In most mess halls and cafeterias, food refilling is initiated only after containers are nearly empty, leading to:

• Unnecessary waiting time
• Service delays during peak hours
• Inefficient staff response

💡 Our Solution

We developed an IoT-based automated food level monitoring system that proactively alerts the kitchen before depletion occurs.

⚙️ Key Features

• Real-time food level detection using ultrasonic sensors
• Wireless alert transmission to the main mess
• Proactive refill mechanism to reduce waiting time
• Scalable architecture for future IoT/AI integration

📊 Expected Impact

Our analysis shows that reducing even 5 minutes of wait time per meal can save approximately 240 hours (~10 days) over a student’s academic tenure — highlighting the practical value of smart automation in everyday campus systems.
